JunWei Song notes!
==================================

JunWei is a Security Researcher at Telecom Technology Center, Taiwan. A paranoid Pythonista who focuses on cybersecurity, reverse engineering, and malware analysis. And as a CPython contributor, PyCon TW Review Board, presented at HITBSecConf, Europython, PyCon Taiwan, PyCon Korea, PyCon Malaysia. He’s the co-founder of Quark-Engine and a security research group, TWBGC.
